# Vrinda Store Annual Sales Report (Excel Dashboard)

This project analyzes the 2022 sales data of Vrinda Store to help understand customer behavior and improve sales in 2023. I cleaned and explored the dataset in Excel, answered business questions, and created an interactive dashboard using pivot tables, charts, and slicers.

## Key Insights Covered:

. Comparison of Sales vs Orders using a combined line-column chart

. Top 5 states contributing to sales

. Customer breakdown by Age & Gender

. Sales distribution across sales channels

. Order status analysis (Delivered, Returned, Cancelled, etc.)

. Sales comparison between Men vs Women

## Techniques Used:

. Excel Pivot Tables

. Slicers & Filters

. Pivot Charts

. Data Cleaning & Formatting

Dashboard Designing

This project helped me understand how Excel dashboards can turn raw data into clear, actionable insights.
